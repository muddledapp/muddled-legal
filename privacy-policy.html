Muddled Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 28, 2026

1. Introduction

Muddled ("we", "our", "us") is a personal bar management app that helps you track your spirits, discover cocktails, and get AI-powered recommendations. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

2. Data We Collect

Account Data

  • Name and email address (on registration)
  • Encrypted password (never stored in plain text)

Bar Inventory Data

  • Bottle names, brands and categories you add
  • Fill levels you set manually

Usage Data

  • Cocktails you view and search
  • Bruno AI chat conversations
  • Weather-based suggestions requested

Location Data

  • Approximate location (city level only)
  • Used only for weather-based cocktail suggestions
  • Never stored permanently on our servers
  • Only collected with your explicit permission

Taste Profile

  • Spirit preferences you select in the quiz
  • Flavour preferences you select in the quiz
  • Used only to personalise Bruno's suggestions

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to:

  • Provide personalised cocktail suggestions
  • Power Bruno AI bartender conversations
  • Show weather-appropriate recommendations
  • Remember your bar inventory across sessions
  • Improve the app experience

We do NOT:

  • Sell your data to third parties
  • Use your data for advertising
  • Share your data with marketers
  • Store your location permanently

4. Third Party Services

We use these services to power Muddled:

  • OpenAI (GPT-4) — Powers Bruno AI conversations and bottle identification. Privacy Policy
  • OpenWeatherMap — Provides weather data for cocktail suggestions. Privacy Policy
  • Sentry — Crash reporting and error tracking to improve stability. Privacy Policy
  • Railway — Cloud hosting for our backend servers. Privacy Policy
  • TheCocktailDB — Cocktail recipe and ingredient database.

5. Data Storage & Security

  • Your data is stored on secure cloud servers
  • Passwords are encrypted using bcrypt (never stored in plain text)
  • Authentication uses JWT tokens that expire automatically
  • All data transfer is encrypted via HTTPS
  • We use Redis for temporary caching only

6. Data Retention

  • Account data: kept until you delete your account
  • Chat history: kept per session
  • Location data: never permanently stored
  • Crash reports: 90 days
  • Cached data: automatically expires (24-72 hours)

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data by contacting us
  • Delete your account and all associated data via Settings → Profile → Delete Account
  • Opt out of location access by denying permission or revoking it in iOS Settings
  • Retake or skip the taste quiz at any time via your Profile
  • Export your data by contacting us

8. Children's Privacy

Muddled is an alcohol-related app intended for users of legal drinking age in their country (21+ in the US). We verify age at first launch. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under legal drinking age. If you believe a minor has created an account, please contact us immediately.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via the app or email. Continued use of Muddled after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
